Rayzel Danser

Soon to be author and a lover of written works.

Born and raised in Colorado I have always loved to read and write. With an over active imagination as a kid, I would always create stories and characters in my free time. I never got serious about writing utill highschool when my friends and I would spend our free time creating powerpoints of random characters. As we created them I always thought that they need a home their own little world to live in. Everytime we made a new powerpoint I would create a story just for them and that led to the creation of my very first book. Avarice and Enigma. Based in a fantasy world, the very last characters I ever made with my highschool friends became the turning point in what I wanted to do when I finally graduated highschool. It was the very first book I actually took the time to sit down and write in memory of those 3 boys who helped me create my characters and story line.
